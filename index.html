Delete My Data - New Creation Teachings App

If you wish to permanently delete your data from our databse, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the New Creation Teachings App on your device.
  2. Tap on the Gear icon located at the bottom right corner of the screen.
  3. In the Settings menu, tap on last item which says "Delete Account"

Please be aware that deleting your account and data is irreversible, and you will lose all your information associated with the app. If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our support team.
